About

Lionel “Bud” Coup (1929-2011) spent the last two decades of his life engaging with the greatest minds in literary criticism in discussion of books he felt did not quite live up to the mark they had set for themselves. The fruit of his uncompromising literary scrupulousness was the BI-DECADAL PEOPLE’S REVIEW, a showcase for treasured negative book reviews hunted down and re-issued by Lionel 20 years after their original date of publication. His spirit lives on in these pages.

NOTE TO THE READER: If you are the author of a 20-year-or-older book review you would like to share, or if you happened to have corresponded with Lionel about a book that you both did not enjoy much, please contact Gary Lemus at gc.lemus@gmail.com. Thank you and God bless.
