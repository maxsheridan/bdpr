Maeve Binchy, 86, Calls It Quits

Lionel hated Maeve Binchy, and he detested the way hapless reviewers stumbled into the trap of extolling her less-than-average literary skills. He once mentioned finding an eviscerating out-of-print review of The Glass Lake in the Financial Times, which he intended to republish, but I haven’t come across it yet. It wasn’t filed in among the Bs. Perhaps Lioniel had a pet name for Binchy and I will discover it in some other drawer or box. Or perhaps it is lost forever. Forgive my pessimism.

If anyone comes across a particularly wounding review of The Glass Lake by Maeve Binchy, please send it to me. You can find my address on the info page.
