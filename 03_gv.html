Gore Vidal: 111 Unanswered Letters

A tragic coincidence! This morning in my office I came across a BDPR box that was filled with unanswered letters written to Gore Vidal over the years and returned to Lionel from various addresses around the globe. By my count there are 111, but there could be more. I don’t know if including excerpts from these on the blog would require the permission of the Gore Vidal estate. Before I post these gems, I need to check into it.
